About Me

I am a passionate pure mathematician in the world of cryptography. During my PhD, I have worked on designing secure outsourcing protocols for verifiable computations, under the guidance of Prof. R Jothi Ramalingam. Post my PhD, I have worked with Dr. R Kabaleeshwaran designing and developing secure post quantum digital signature from lattices. I am currently working as an Institute Postdoctoral fellow with Prof. Monosij Maitra at Indian Institute of Technology, Kharagpur.
